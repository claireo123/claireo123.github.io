Getting There

If you haven't visited the area before, here's some useful tips and info.

Overnight parking is available on the premises, but like most places cars are left at the owner's risk.

A list of taxi firms in the area are listed here, so if you'd like to leave your car and collect it the next day you can do.

Taxis must be booked in advance, especially for pick up after the wedding. There are loads of firms based in Bideford & Barnstable but some local companies include:

The closest train station to the venue is Barnstable, which can be reached by changing at Exeter St Davids.

National Express also run coaches from London to Bideford, Barnstaple, Braunton, Westward Ho! and Ilfracombe.

If you're having any issues getting to Devon or the venue, please let us know and we can help you find a good route.

Where to Stay

There is no accommodation directly on site, but the area around North Devon is beautiful and well set up for visitors.

There are a lot of places to stay, and we recommend searching in Bideford, Barnstable, Appledore, Westward Ho! and beyond. These areas are all a short cab or car journey away.

Book Soon!

If possible please book early to avoid disappointment as it looks like a lot of people will be staycationing this year.

We suggest you try to make sure that your bookings have cancellation or postponement options as a lot could still change in the months ahead.
